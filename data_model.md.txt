# Data Model

## Overview

The solution uses a star-style analytical model built on Microsoft Fabric Lakehouse tables.

---

## Tables

### financial_news_raw

Stores the original news articles collected from the Finnhub API.

Primary Key:
Article_ID

---

### financial_news_clean

Cleaned version of the raw news data.

Primary Key:
Article_ID

---

### financial_news_sentiment

Stores AI-generated sentiment predictions.

Primary Key:
Article_ID

Important fields:

- Sentiment
- Confidence
- Sentiment Score

---

### ticker_market_intelligence

Aggregated sentiment metrics at ticker level.

Primary Key:
Ticker

Includes:

- Positive %
- Negative %
- Main Topic
- Recommendation
- Risk

---

### portfolio_positions

Current investment holdings imported from the Investment Lakehouse.

Primary Key:
Ticker

Includes:

- Current Shares
- Average Cost
- Market Value
- Unrealized Profit

---

### stock_prices

Current market prices retrieved from Yahoo Finance.

Primary Key:
Ticker

---

### portfolio_assistant_signals

Final AI-generated recommendations combining portfolio positions and market intelligence.

Primary Key:
Ticker

Includes:

- Recommendation
- Confidence
- Risk
- Assistant Response

---

## Relationships

financial_news_clean
→ financial_news_sentiment
